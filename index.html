The 3-Phase Method

We Fix The Root Cause. Not Just The Symptom.

Consistency matters more than exercise type. But neither works if you're training dysfunction. Our method follows the clinical evidence proximal stability first, motor control before load, graded exposure to feared movements, and progressive overload that your body is actually ready for.

01

Foundation Calm, Educate & Stabilize

Pain Neuroscience Education (100200 min over weeks), isometrics for the affected area to reduce cortical inhibition, and proximal stability work (core out). We let inflammation settle while building capacity not atrophying.

02

Strengthen Load With Purpose

Motor control is established. Now we layer in progressive loading bilateral to unilateral, partial ROM to full, graded exposure to your feared movements. Pain up to 35/10 is acceptable if it returns to baseline within 24 hours. Fear-avoidance is addressed head-on.

03

Perform Strength, Hypertrophy & Function

You're not just pain-free you're building toward a goal. Customized programming for hypertrophy, strength, or sport performance, with objective progression criteria so nothing is arbitrary. This is where most coaches start. We save it for when you're ready.
